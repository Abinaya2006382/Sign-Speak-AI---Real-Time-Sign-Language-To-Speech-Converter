Project Title:
SignSpeak AI – Real-Time Sign Language to Speech Converter
Problem Statement:
Communication between hearing-impaired individuals and non-sign-language users is often difficult. An automated system is needed to translate sign language into speech instantly.
Objectives:
Detect hand gestures in real time.
Convert sign language into text.
Convert generated text into speech.
Improve communication accessibility.
Scope:
Educational institutions
Public services
Healthcare communication
Daily interactions
